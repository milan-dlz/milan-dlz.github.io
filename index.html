WELCOME TO THE
SMART GARBAGE PROJECT

You may be wondering: what is the Smart Garbage project really?
Well, look no further. On this page, we'll explain just what it is...
... and why you should be excited about it.

Why this project?

Nowadays, companies face pressure on environmental awareness, rational waste management, and corporate social responsibility. At the same time, they are facing cost reduction demands. This project attempts to answer this challenge by offering an innovation that facilitates waste management, reducing certain costs at the same time, and making companies environmentally friendly. The main purpose of this project is to design and build a small intelligent garbage sorter which could stand next to a drink dispenser in a company. Smart Garbage is a sorting robot. As recycling is an important part of sustainable development, the Smart Garbage will facilitate the rational management of waste, as well as reduce costs, for example the costs of segregation, supply chains and transportation. It is also an innovation based on modern technology that allows company development. Furthermore, it is embedded in the CSR perspective making the company environmentally friendly which will increase the interest of stakeholders.

See this little box right here? This is the Smart Garbage Sorter. This convenient device helps its users sorts their garbage, like its name implies.

So, how does it work?

  1. You first place your trash in the socket.
  2. Then, the machine compresses it.
  3. While it does that, it analyses the trash thanks to its different sensors.
  4. Then, the trash falls into the device, where it lands on a slide.
  5. Finally, the slide bends over to the left or the right depending on the type of trash...
  6. ... which leads the trash to fall into the appropriate bin.
